OUR SERVICES

MUSIC LESSONS

Professional instruction in piano, guitar, violin, voice, drums, and more. From beginners to advanced students, we tailor each lesson to your skill level and musical goals.

LANGUAGE TUTORING

Master English, Japanese, Korean, Thai, Malay and more with native and certified instructors. Conversational practice, grammar, writing, and test preparation available.

Learn programming from scratch or advance your skills. Python, JavaScript, Web Development, Mobile Apps, Game Development, and more. Perfect for kids, teens, and adults.

OUR EXPERIENCE
OUR APPROACH
OUR COMMITMENT

With over 10 years of experience in music education, language instruction, and technology training, VIK&RHYME has helped thousands of students achieve their learning goals.

Our instructors are certified professionals with degrees in their respective fields and a passion for teaching. We combine traditional pedagogy with modern teaching methods to deliver engaging and effective lessons.

We believe every student learns differently. That's why we create personalized learning plans tailored to your goals, learning style, and pace.

Our curriculum is designed to be practical and enjoyable, incorporating real-world applications, interactive exercises, and creative projects that keep students motivated and engaged.

We are committed to providing accessible, affordable, and high-quality education for learners of all ages and backgrounds.

From your first lesson to achieving mastery, we're with you every step of the way. Our support extends beyond scheduled sessions with practice resources, feedback, and ongoing guidance.
